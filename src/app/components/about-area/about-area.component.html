Engaging New Audiences through Smart Approach

Incorporated in 1983, Strategic Investment Limited (SIL) is a private equity and portfolio investment firm that is focused on the promotion of new ventures, the identification and acquisition of undervalued companies, and the investment in listed and unlisted companies.

In pursuing our objectives, we explore the investment environment to identify strategic opportunities for the creation of new businesses, seeks collaboration with reputable companies for the exploitation of identified opportunities, and analyses both listed an unlisted equity markets to identify undervalued companies for the acquisition of majority or minority stakes.
