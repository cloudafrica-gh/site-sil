Incredible Airline

Africa World Airlines Limited (AWA) is a Ghanaian registered private sector company which has been in operation for 5 ½ years now. It has the sole vision of making air travel the best option for travellers within the markets in which we operate.

AWA was incorporated on November 15, 2010, received its Air Carrier License (ACL) from Ghana Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA) in March 2011, and launched its maiden flight on September 21, 2012.

AWA now has a fleet of six (6) modern ERJ 145LR jets, and plans to acquire more and bigger aircraft this year. AWA operates flights to four (4) cities in Ghana namely Accra, Kumasi, Tamale and Takoradi, Lagos and Abuja in Nigeria as well as Monrovia in Liberia. The company plans to reach all of West Africa by the end of the year and launch our first intercontinental flight to London next year.
