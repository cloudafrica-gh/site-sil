Innovation, Investment & Inclusion

Fintech is vital to connect millions of African mobile users towards social services such as sports betting and ensuring subscribers have a one-stop shop to meet their daily needs - remittances, bill payments, airtime, donations, sports betting, etc.

Cloud Africa is a financial technology company established in 2015 with a strong focus on the mobile payment and value added services (VAS) space across Africa. The company is strategizing to actively engage in the following areas - mobile payments, content aggregation and content distribution (SMS, MMS, data services) in partnership with the mobile carriers across Africa. It is a registered VAS provider and holds a number of interconnections in order to monetise third party and proprietary mobile services.

Mobile payments is a primary focus for the UK company which will soon launch our mobile wallet under the FastCash Africa brand. Cloud Africa has local offices in many African countries including Ghana, Zambia, Nigeria, Sierra Leone, Zimbabwe, and Kenya. The company envisages being present in over 15 African markets by Q1 2019.
