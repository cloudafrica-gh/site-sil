Incredible Infrastructure

Lighting & Construction Africa Co. Ltd (L&C Africa) supports the development agenda of Ghana by exploiting opportunities in the renewable energy space across the country. With the support of its Italian consultants, Ambiente e Paesaggio, the company has studied the potentials of nine (9) mini-hydro power sites in the Volta Region.

L&C Africa has completed feasibility studies and the Phase I of the mini-hydro development project, the construction of dams at the three (3) most promising sites, will commence in the third quarter of 2018.

The company have identified sites in the Upper West Region, that will have its first solar power projects.
