Financial Strategist

SAS, an integrated investment banking and financial advisory services firm, with a commitment to provide quality service to private and institutional investors in Ghana and other emerging markets. Through its two (2) subsidiary companies, Strategic African Securities Ltd and SAS Investment Management Ltd, SAS provides securities trading, corporate finance advisory, investment research and asset management services.

Among the high profile investment banking/corporate finance transactions executedby SAS are the Initial Public Offering (IPO) and the Rights Issue of Ghana Commercial Bank Ltd and Golden Star Resources, the sale of GNPC’s shares in Ecobank Ghana Ltd and the issue of the Government of Ghana’s Golden Jubilee Savings Bonds.

SAS also played lead roles in many acquisitions, including the acquisition of Mobil Oil Ghana Limited by Total Ghana Limited, followed by a merger of the two companies to form Total Petroleum Ghana Limited. It was also the key advisor in the merger between Guinness Ghana Limited and Ghana Breweries Limited to form Guinness Ghana Breweries Limited.
