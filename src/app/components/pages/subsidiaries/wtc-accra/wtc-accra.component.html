A Masterpiece in the Sky

World Trade Centre Accra (WTC Accra) has established an iconic world-class landmark for international business that would make a lasting impact on the local economy and enhance prosperity.

WTC Accra’s mission is to create a one-stop shop for international trade and investment through a platform of expert services and world class office space; provide linkages between global and local businesses through the worldwide World Trade Centers Association (WTCA) network; and forge alliances to enhance economic growth.

WTC Accra has placed all services associated with global commerce under one roof. It is bringing together businesses and government agencies involved in international trade, providing essential trade services and helping to stimulate the economy of Ghana.
